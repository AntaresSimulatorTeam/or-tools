Note(user): we lose a bit by returning a BeginEndWrapper<> on top of this iterator rather than a simple IntegerRange<> on the arc indices.

On my computer: around 420M arcs/sec instead of 440M arcs/sec.

However, it is slightly more consistent to do it this way, and we don't have two different codes depending on the way a client iterates on the arcs.
